About me

I have an Associates of Science degree in Computer Science. My initial programming experience was with C++ which was very challenging but a lot of fun. I learned to build several projects including a tic tac toe played against a computer game along with a text based adventure game in C++.

I have enjoyed learning HTML, CSS, and JavaScript because I feel it is more creative than C++. I am a creative person and I like expressing my creativity through programming. One thing I enjoy about programming is that there is no one single right way to complete a task, there are many ways to accomplish a task but everyone codes differently.

From having very minimal experience with coding before the AS degree, I learned that I am capable of learning anything if I really put my mind to it. I learn well on my own but also will seek help when I absolutely need to. Programming has also taught me analytical thinking skills along with patience.

When I am not learning new coding skills, I like to spend my free time baking desserts, especially cookies. I also enjoy going to the movies, playing with my two orange cats, and doing fun activities with my sisters.
